## 🔥 What is this W App? 

Hadist Pedia is your daily plug for authentic Islamic teachings. We took the heavy, dense texts of monumental works like *Shahih al-Bukhari* and wrapped them up in a slick, modern, and highly scrollable UI. 

Whether you're doing a quick daily read to center your soul or doing a deep dive into the *isnad* (chain of narrators) and *tafsir* (exegesis) because you're built different, this app has your back. Real talk, learning your deen has never looked this good.

## ✨ Core Features That Slap

- **Hadist of the Day**: A dynamic carousel serving you fresh, categorized ahadith daily. Swipe through it like your favorite feed, but make it spiritual. 📿
- **Immaculate Dark Mode & Glassmorphism**: High-contrast, clean typography, neon-green accents, and frosted glass elements. The UI is literally stunning.
- **Deep Lore (Isnad & Asbab al-Wurud)**: Not just the translation! We hit you with the context, the meaning, the narrators' bios, and relevant Quranic Ayats. 
- **Read More / Less Flex**: Expandable text fields because sometimes you just want the TL;DR, and sometimes you want the full scholarly tea. ☕

## 🛠 Tech Stack Vibes

This codebase was cooked up using:
- **Flutter & Dart**: For that native feel on any screen.
- **GoRouter**: Clean and stateful navigation.
- **JSON Data Sorcery**: Dynamic parsing that makes updating content a literal breeze. 

## 🚀 How to Run (Don't Sleep on This)

1. Clone this repo like a true dev.
2. Run `flutter pub get` to grab those dependencies.
3. Hook up your emulator or physical device.
4. Hit `flutter run` and watch the magic unfold on your screen.
